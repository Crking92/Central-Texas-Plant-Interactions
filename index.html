How to read the scores

Ecological score estimates the documented direct animal-use signal. Balanced score adds evidence strength, target-zone confidence, and recommendation caution. A low score usually means verified data are thin for that category; it does not mean the plant has no ecological value.

How to read this dashboard

Direct animal-plant interactions only
Important: this dashboard is not saying these are the only animals that use each plant. It shows interactions that are currently present in verified or source-backed records.
  • Birds, Lepidoptera, and bees are shown first in plant detail pages.
  • Ecological score shows direct animal-use signal.
  • Balanced score adds evidence, locality, and recommendation confidence.
  • Non-native or invasive flags affect recommendation status.
